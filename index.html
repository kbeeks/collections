A COLLECTION OF WHISTLE PIGS
AT THE BEEKHUIS HUIS

2021 has seen a surge of marmot activity in Allanburg, Ontario - specifically at the Beekhuis homestead. Bill Murray the groundhog (or whistle pig, land beaver, or whatever your preferred nickname is for the mighty groundhog) made his first appearance in spring, followed by his true love Billhemina and a few months later, a litter of six little minis. This happy little family has made appearances throughout the summer and fall, enjoying the bounty of greens available and preparing for hibernation.
